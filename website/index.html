Overview

Manage every part of your center in one place

The layout is simple, but the platform is complete. Your team gets one connected system for patient care, field work, supplies, access control, and training.

Care

Patients and home visits stay connected

Maintain comprehensive patient records including medical history, care plans, and real-time status updates. Plan, schedule, and track home-based care visits for your field team with reminders and visit logs.

Supply

Equipment and medicine are easier to track

Manage your equipment inventory, know what is in stock, what has been distributed, and what must be returned. Log every medicine supply with a clear audit trail across your care network.

Team

Volunteers, caregivers, and staff work with clarity

Coordinate volunteers and caregivers, assign tasks, track participation, and communicate efficiently. Fine-grained role-based access ensures every team member sees only what they need.

Core Features

Everything your care team needs

From patient registration to volunteer coordination, the platform covers every operational need of a modern palliative care center.

01

Patient Management

Maintain comprehensive patient records including medical history, care plans, and real-time status updates. Role-based access ensures sensitive data stays secure.

02

Home Visit Scheduling

Plan, schedule, and track home-based care visits for your field team. Never miss a visit with built-in reminders and visit logs.

03

Equipment Tracking

Manage your entire medical equipment inventory. Know what is in stock, what has been distributed, and what needs to be returned in real time.

04

Medicine Distribution

Log and track every medicine supply given to patients. Maintain a clear audit trail of medication distribution across your care network.

05

Volunteer Coordination

Manage and coordinate volunteers and caregivers who support patient care. Assign tasks, track participation, and communicate efficiently.

06

Role-Based Access Control

Fine-grained access management ensures every team member sees only what they need, keeping patient data safe and workflows smooth.
